About the NTC Internship Program

Nepal Telecom offers a unique internship experience for students and fresh graduates in the fields of Technical, Software, and Admin/HR. Our program is designed to provide real-world exposure, hands-on learning, and professional growth in a dynamic telecom environment.

  • Technical: Work with network infrastructure, telecom hardware, and learn from industry experts.
  • Software: Develop and deploy real applications, APIs, and automation tools for NTC.
  • Admin/HR: Get involved in HR processes, policy design, and organizational development.

All interns receive mentorship, access to exclusive resources, and the opportunity to contribute to national-scale projects. Outstanding performers may receive certificates, recommendations, and even job offers!
